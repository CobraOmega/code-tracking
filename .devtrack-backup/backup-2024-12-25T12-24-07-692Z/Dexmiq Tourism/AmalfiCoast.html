Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Amalfi

• Arrive at the Amalfi Coast and transfer to your hotel.

• Evening: Relax and enjoy a welcome dinner featuring local Italian cuisine.

Day 2: Positano & Ravello

• Morning: Visit the picturesque town of Positano, strolling through its narrow streets.

• Afternoon: Explore Ravello and its beautiful gardens.

Day 3: Pompeii & Ancient Ruins

• Morning: Guided tour of the ancient ruins of Pompeii.

• Afternoon: Explore nearby villages and historical sites.

• Evening: Enjoy a traditional Italian dinner.

Day 4: Island of Capri

Morning: Day trip to the island of Capri.

Afternoon: Explore the island's famed Blue Grotto and luxury shops.

Evening: Dinner at a waterfront restaurant.

Day 5: Scenic Coastal Tour

• Morning: Scenic drive along the Amalfi Coast, visiting hidden beaches.

• Afternoon: Leisure time for shopping or relaxing.

• Evening: Special seafood dinner overlooking the coast.

Day 6: Cooking Class & Wine-Tasting

• Morning: Participate in a hands-on cooking class

• Afternoon: Enjoy a wine-tasting session at a local vineyard.

• Evening: Relax with a final dinner in a charming coastal town.

Day 7: Departure

• Enjoy breakfast and transfer to the airport for your flight home.
