Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Lakeside Views

Arrive at Lake Louise and check into your lakeside resort.

Evening: Relax and enjoy a dinner with views of the serene lake.

Day 2: Explore Lake Louise

Morning: Guided hike around Lake Louise to take in the views.

Afternoon: Enjoy a leisurely boat ride on the lake or relax by the shore.

Evening: Dinner at a cozy lodge overlooking the lake.

Day 3: Skiing or Snowboarding (Winter)

Morning: After breakfast, enjoy a ski or snowboarding lesson at the nearby resort.

Afternoon: Free time for skiing or exploring the surrounding trails.

Evening: Warm up with a traditional Canadian dinner at a lodge.

Day 4: Banff National Park Tour

Morning: Visit Banff National Park for scenic views and wildlife spotting.

Afternoon: Take a guided tour of the charming town of Banff.

Evening: Dinner at a local restaurant in Banff.

Day 5: Departure

Enjoy a leisurely breakfast and relax before transferring to the airport.
