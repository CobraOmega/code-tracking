Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Coorg

Arrive in Coorg and transfer to your resort amidst the coffee plantations

Evening: Relax with a cup of freshly brewed coffee and enjoy the tranquil surroundings.

Day 2: Explore Coffee Estates & Nature Walks

Morning: Visit a local coffee estate and learn about the coffee-growing process.

Afternoon: Take a nature walk or a light trek through the lush hills of Coorg.

Evening: Enjoy a traditional Coorgi dinner with local specialties.

Day 3: Leisure & Departure

Morning: Spend the morning relaxing in the scenic surroundings.

Afternoon: Transfer to the airport for your departure.
