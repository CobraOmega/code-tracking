Discover the serene beauty of Kerala’s backwaters with a tranquil houseboat journey. This peaceful retreat offers the chance to float along the lush, palm-lined canals, enjoy traditional Kerala cuisine, and witness the unique charm of rural Kerala. A perfect getaway for nature lovers and those seeking relaxation.

Highlights

  • Cruise along the picturesque backwaters in a traditional Kerala houseboat
  • Visit local villages and witness the lifestyle of Kerala's backwater residents
  • Enjoy traditional Kerala meals prepared on board
  • Experience the beauty of Kuttanadu and its unique ecosystem

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Alleppey

Arrive in Alleppey and board your traditional Kerala houseboat.

Evening: Enjoy a leisurely cruise along the backwaters and savor a freshly prepared Kerala dinner.

Day 2: Cruise & Explore Villages

Morning: Continue your cruise through the backwaters, passing lush paddy fields and coconut groves.

Afternoon: Visit local villages, see the traditional way of life, and experience Kerala’s rural charm.

Evening: Enjoy a sunset dinner on board while drifting along tranquil waters.

Day 3: Return & Departure

• Morning: Enjoy breakfast on board before returning to Alleppey.

• Afternoon: Transfer to the airport for your departure.

What's Not Included
