Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Goa

Arrive in Goa and transfer to your beachfront resort.

Evening: Relax on the beach and enjoy a sunset dinner by the shore.

Day 2: Explore Goa's Beaches

Morning: Visit popular beaches like Baga and Anjuna.

Afternoon: Engage in water sports such as parasailing, jet-skiing, or windsurfing.

Evening: Enjoy a beachfront dinner with live music.

Day 3: Visit Historical Sites & Markets

Morning: Explore the historic churches and convents of Old Goa, including the Basilica of Bom Jesus.

Afternoon: Visit the vibrant markets of Mapusa or Calangute for shopping.

Evening: Enjoy Goan seafood at a local restaurant.

Day 4: Leisure & Departure

Morning: Relax by the beach or indulge in a spa treatment.

Afternoon: Transfer to the airport for your departure.
