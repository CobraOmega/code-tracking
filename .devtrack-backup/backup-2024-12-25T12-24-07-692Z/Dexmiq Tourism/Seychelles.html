Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Seychelles

• Arrive in Seychelles and transfer to your beachfront villa.

• Evening: Enjoy a welcome dinner overlooking the ocean.

Day 2: Island Exploration & Beach Relaxation

• Morning: Explore the famous islands of Praslin and La Digue.

• Afternoon: Relax on the beaches of Anse Lazio or Anse Source d'Argent.

• Evening: Dinner at a luxury resort featuring fresh seafood

Day 3: Snorkeling & Diving Adventure

• Morning: Take a snorkeling excursion to explore vibrant coral reefs.

• Afternoon: Optional diving trip to see diverse marine life.

• Evening: Sunset dinner on a private boat.

Day 4: Nature Reserves & Spa Experience

• Morning: Visit the Vallée de Mai Nature Reserve, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

• Afternoon: Indulge in a tropical spa experience with views of the ocean.

• Evening: Dinner at a resort with local Creole cuisine.

Day 5: Scenic Coastal Tour

• Morning: Scenic drive along the Amalfi Coast, visiting hidden beaches.

• Afternoon: Leisure time for shopping or relaxing.

• Evening: Special seafood dinner overlooking the coast.

Day 6: Cooking Class & Wine-Tasting

• Morning: Participate in a hands-on cooking class

• Afternoon: Enjoy a wine-tasting session at a local vineyard.

• Evening: Relax with a final dinner in a charming coastal town.

Day 7: Departure

• Enjoy breakfast and transfer to the airport for your flight home.
