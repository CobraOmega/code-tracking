Zen & Cherry Blossoms

⭐ 4.5/5 (375 reviews)

Immerse yourself in the tranquil beauty of Kyoto, Japan's ancient capital. Known for its traditional wooden houses, serene temples, and stunning cherry blossoms, Kyoto is a city that blends history, culture, and nature in perfect harmony. This package offers a peaceful retreat surrounded by Japan's natural beauty and timeless culture.

Highlights

  • Visit iconic temples and shrines, including Kinkaku-ji and Fushimi Inari
  • Experience the beauty of Kyoto's cherry blossoms (seasonal)
  • Enjoy a traditional Japanese tea ceremony
  • Stroll through peaceful gardens and zen rock gardens

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Kyoto

• Arrive in Kyoto and transfer to your hotel..

• Evening: Enjoy a traditional Japanese dinner at a local restaurant..

Day 2: Explore Temples & Gardens

• Morning: Visit the Golden Pavilion (Kinkaku-ji) and its beautiful gardens.

• Afternoon: Explore the zen rock garden at Ryoan-ji and stroll through the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.

• Evening: Traditional Kyoto dinner at a kaiseki restaurant.

Day 3: Cherry Blossom Viewing (Seasonal)

• Morning: Visit Maruyama Park and other famous cherry blossom spots..

• Afternoon: Free time for shopping or exploring the charming Gion district.

• Evening: Relax with a tea ceremony and enjoy a dinner overlooking the tranquil Kamogawa River.

Day 4: Fushimi Inari & Tea Ceremony

• Morning: Visit the famous Fushimi Inari Shrine with its iconic torii gates.

• Afternoon: Enjoy a traditional tea ceremony experience.

Dinner at a local restaurant featuring Kyoto-style kaiseki cuisine.

Day 5: Cultural Exploration

• Morning: Visit the Nijo Castle, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

• Afternoon: Take a leisurely stroll through the Philosopher's Path.

• Evening: Enjoy a quiet dinner at a Kyoto garden restaurant.

Day 6: Departure

• Morning: Participate in a hands-on cooking class
