Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Bora Bora

Arrive at Bora Bora and be greeted by your representative. Transfer to your overwater bungalow and relax with a welcome drink. In the evening, enjoy a welcome dinner at the resort’s fine dining restaurant with stunning views of the lagoon.

Day 2: Exploring Bora Bora

Morning: After breakfast, enjoy a guided snorkeling tour to explore vibrant coral reefs.

Afternoon: Free time for water sports like kayaking or simply relax on the beach.

Evening: Experience a romantic sunset dinner overlooking the lagoon.

Day 3: Lagoon Safari & Spa Day

Morning: Embark on a Lagoon Safari tour, including shark and ray feeding and a boat ride.

Afternoon: Relax with a 1-hour spa treatment.

Evening: Enjoy a private, intimate dinner in your bungalow.

Day 4: Adventure & Leisure

Morning: Explore Bora Bora on a half-day boat excursion to secluded islands.

Afternoon: Participate in a cooking class or learn traditional Tahitian dance.

Evening: End the day with a private dinner by the water.

Day 5: Departure

Enjoy a leisurely breakfast before transferring to the airport for your flight home.
