Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Udaipur

Arrive in Udaipur and transfer to your royal heritage hotel.

vening: Enjoy a traditional Rajasthani dinner at the hotel.

Day 2: Explore the City Palace & Lake Pichola

Morning: Visit the City Palace, one of the largest royal residences in Rajasthan.

Afternoon: Enjoy a boat ride on Lake Pichola, passing by the Jag Mandir.
