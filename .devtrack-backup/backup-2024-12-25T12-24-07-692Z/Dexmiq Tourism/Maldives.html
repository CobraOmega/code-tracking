Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Maldives

Arrive at the Maldives and be transferred to your overwater villa.

Evening: Relax with a welcome drink, followed by dinner overlooking the pristine beach.

Day 2: Water Adventure

Morning: After breakfast, enjoy snorkeling and explore vibrant coral reefs.

Afternoon: Relax by the beach or opt for a jet-skiing adventure.

Evening: Enjoy a sunset cruise, followed by a seafood dinner at the resort.

Day 3: Underwater Experience & Spa

Morning: Enjoy a guided diving tour of the Maldives’ underwater world.

Afternoon: Indulge in a couple's spa treatment with ocean views.

Evening: Dinner under the stars with a private beachfront setup.

Day 4: Leisure & Culture

Morning: Participate in a traditional Maldivian cooking class or cultural experience.

Afternoon: Spend the day at leisure, enjoying water sports or sunbathing.

Evening: A special seafood dinner at a luxury underwater restaurant.

Day 5: Departure

Enjoy a leisurely breakfast before transferring to the airport for your flight home.
